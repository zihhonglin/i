About Zih-Hong


Hello! Good to see you.


My name is Zih-Hong, I'm now studying Interaction Design at Graduate Institute of Design of Taiwan-Tech, focusing on the research of HCI field during my study period. Learning how to explore the needs of users and solving the pain points through design has always been my main goal. I had submitted 4 pieces of conference papers related to UX and HCI at present and positively participate in the activities of UX field.

As an interaction designer, I'm good at thinking from the angle of users, planning and drawing up the friendly operating interface and flow; I also have the development experiences of App & website at the same time. Those ability above allows me to produce the prototype rapidly and move on to usability test. In a nutshell, I'm a designer with cross-field thinking and integrated abilities.
